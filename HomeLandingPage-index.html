Parish Mission Preview

United in love, service and collaboration, we the Parish of St. John Bosco - Tondo, form a Christ-centered and missionary community, with preferential ministry for the youth, the family and the marginalized, Inspired by St. John Bosco and guided by Mary, Help of Christians, we undertake the path of New Evangelization through lifelong faith formation, meaningful liturgical celebrations, uplifting social services and joyful community building. All for the greater glory of the Triune God.

About the Parish

St. John Bosco Parish Tondo started in 1967 with the first Salesian mission in the area, followed by the Don Bosco Youth Center in 1968, and its canonical erection as a parish in 1969. The Salesians formed the mission to address the educational and spiritual needs of the youth in Tondo. Through the years, the parish supported young people through academic training, vocational instruction, and faith formation programs that focused on their growth and development.
