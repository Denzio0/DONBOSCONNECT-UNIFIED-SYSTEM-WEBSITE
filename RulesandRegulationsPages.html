RULES AND REGULATIONS

St. John Bosco Parish upholds a set of guidelines to ensure that all church activities, ceremonies, and services are conducted with respect, order, and reverence. We kindly ask all parishioners and visitors to observe these rules to maintain the solemnity of our sacred spaces and to promote a peaceful environment for worship.

BE PUNCTUAL

Arrive on time to maintain order and respect for the ceremony.

DON'T LITTER

Keep the surroundings tidy and dispose of waste properly.

KEEP QUIET

Maintain noise to a minimum to preserve the solemnity of the place.

PROPER ATTIRE

Men

Collared Shirt or T-Shirt

Jeans or Slack Pants

Polo Shirt or Long Sleeves Polo

Women

Collared Blouse or Long Sleeve

Corporate Attire/School Uniform

Dresses and Long Gowns

AVOID WEARING THE FOLLOWING DURING MASS

Cap or Sombrero

Jersey or Sando

Shorts

Spaghetti Strap or Tank Top

Skirt or Skimpy Shorts

Sleeveless or Plunging Neckline
