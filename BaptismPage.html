What Is Baptism?

Baptism is a sacred Christian sacrament in which a person is welcomed into the faith through water. It symbolizes cleansing, rebirth, and the beginning of a new life in Christ.

Why Is Baptism Important?

  • Signifies cleansing of sin and spiritual rebirth
  • Marks formal entry into the Christian faith
  • Strengthens relationship with God
  • Foundation for receiving future sacraments
